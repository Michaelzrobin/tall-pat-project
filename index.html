ABOUT

Tall Pat Records is the brain child of one man and several bottles of beer. Formed in October of 2012 at the Empty Bottle when Tall Pat got drunk and convinced Paul from the Dumpster Babies to let him put out their record. Fast forward a year and Tall Pat Records has emerged as the single most important record label this century, working with the Windy Cities finest rock’n’roll acts. Tall Pat currently lives and parties in the Logan Square neighborhood of Chicago. No Weak Pits.
